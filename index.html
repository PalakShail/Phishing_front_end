What is Phishing? (Simple Explanation)

Phishing ek aisa online fraud hai jahan attacker aapko fake email, message ya website bhejta hai jo asli bank, company, ya college jaisa dikhai deta hai. Unka main goal hota hai:

  • Password churaana (email, social media, bank, etc.)
  • OTP, credit card number, UPI PIN lena
  • Malicious link par click karwana

Usually phishing messages:

  • Bahut urgent tone mein hote hain – “your account will be blocked”, “verify now”
  • Strange ya unknown links hote hain
  • Spelling mistakes ya weird email address hota hai

Phishing Detection Demo (Front-End)

  2. Step 2: Is tool ke textbox mein paste karega.
  3. Step 3: “Check for Phishing” button press karega.
  4. Step 4: Front-end JavaScript kuch simple rules se text ko analyse karega.
  5. Step 5: User ko clear result milega – High / Medium / Low risk + reason.

Basic Safety Tips for Users

  • Always check if the website uses https://, not just http://
  • Never share OTP, PIN or full card number on links from unknown messages.
  • Official banks/colleges usually don’t ask passwords through email or SMS.
  • If a message feels too urgent or scary, double check from official app or website.
